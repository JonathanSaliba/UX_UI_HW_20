About Spontour

Introduction

A travel planning app that helps elevate the pain points involved with planning a trip, either alone or with a group of friends through custom/curated experiences based on the individuals' or groups' interests. The projected consisted the use of the following tools and methodologies, User Research (Interviews + Surveys) & Analysis, Persona Creation, User Experience Design (UI/UX), Wireframing, Prototyping, Remote User Testing, Figma, Marvel, and InVision.
